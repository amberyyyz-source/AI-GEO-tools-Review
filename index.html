⚠ Common Gaps Across All Three

  • Traffic/conversion closed-loop attribution: none of the three can directly equate "AI mentions" with "actual website visits/conversions." Otterly and Peec.ai lack native deep GA4 integration (only export/Looker Studio support); Profound's attribution depends on CDN integration (Akamai/Cloudflare, etc.), which may not work for SMBs or sites without matching architecture. Recommendation: whichever vendor we choose, we'll need to build our own custom channel grouping in GA4 to fill this gap.
  • Google AI Overviews click traffic: this is naturally classified as "organic search" in GA4, and no tool can currently split it out cleanly — this is a GA4 architectural limitation, not a vendor shortcoming.
  • Sentiment analysis reliability needs live verification: Otterly officially claims support but third-party reviews found it missing from the dashboard; Profound's and Peec.ai's sentiment analysis are relatively better regarded in reviews, but we should still require a live demo from all three — no verbal confirmations accepted.
